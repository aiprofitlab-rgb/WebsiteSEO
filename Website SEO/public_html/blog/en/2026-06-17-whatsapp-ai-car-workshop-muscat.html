How Does AI Handle Complex Car Diagnoses?

The AI bot identifies routine maintenance requests and books them immediately. For complex mechanical faults, it collects the symptom data and seamlessly escalates the chat to a senior mechanic for manual review.

You don't want a bot guessing why an engine is knocking. AI excels at structured workflows: oil changes, AC top-ups, and tire alignments. By handling the 80% of routine requests, your expert staff can dedicate their focus entirely to the 20% of high-value, complex diagnostic conversations.
