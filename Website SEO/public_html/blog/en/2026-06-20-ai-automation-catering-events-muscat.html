How does AI automate catering and event inquiries in Muscat?

Direct Answer: AI automates catering and event inquiries by instantly responding to incoming customer requests via WhatsApp and web chat. It checks calendar availability, provides custom menu packages, and collects guest numbers to qualify leads without human delays.

Event planning requires multiple negotiation stages. When a Muscat client submits an inquiry, they expect immediate feedback on dietary restrictions, menus, and costs. Traditionally, managers consult chefs, calculate costs, and check availability—a cycle taking up to 48 hours. By utilizing Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to reference verified documents, a chatbot can instantly answer questions about menus and booking availability using live schedules.

Because the AI assistant connects to the catering company's CRM, it collects essential details like guest counts, event dates, and venue locations. It then suggests custom menu packages based on this data. For a banquet of 250 guests, the AI presents structured package options and offers to schedule a tasting session, transferring qualified leads to the sales team for final closure. How does catering automation comply with Oman's Personal Data Protection Law?

Direct Answer: Catering automation complies with Oman's Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL) by utilizing localized cloud storage, end-to-end data encryption, and role-based access permissions. This prevents unauthorized access to customer contacts, banquet details, and payment histories.

Event planners handle sensitive customer data, including guest lists, dietary restrictions, and transaction details. Under the Omani Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL), established by Royal Decree No. 6/2022, hospitality businesses must protect this data. Our automated systems implement strict security controls. All communication transcripts are encrypted, and data is stored on secure, local cloud servers within Oman's borders to satisfy national data residency regulations.

Moreover, role-based access controls ensure that only authorized personnel can view billing documents. This reduces data leaks and builds trust with clients.
