About AI Profit Lab

We build simple smart systems for businesses in Oman and the GCC. No tech jargon, no pressure. Just honest work.

AI Profit Lab is the technology consulting brand of International Gulf Lotus SPC, registered in the Sultanate of Oman.

Founder & Builder
OUR STORY

A Message from Nahid

"I started AI Profit Lab because I saw business owners across the GCC drowning in repetitive work. Answering the same WhatsApp questions, manually entering customer data, chasing leads—hours every week that could be spent growing their business.

The big tech companies have systems that do this work automatically. But they're expensive, complicated, and built for huge corporations—not for local businesses like yours.

So I decided to build something different. Simple systems that actually work, priced fairly, with no tech jargon. We sit with you (or hop on a call), understand your problems, and build tools that solve them. Nothing more, nothing less.

Today, we're helping businesses save time and increase profits. But our mission is still the same: build simple smart systems for the people who need them most."

Nahid Abyari
Founder, AI Profit Lab
